MUSCLE EXERCISES

To gain muscle,focus on strength training and proper nutrition,here are some exercises

EXERCISES

1. Compound Movements:

Squats: Targets legs and core. Deadlifts: Engages back, legs, and core. Bench Press: Works on chest, shoulders, and triceps. Pull-Ups/Chin-Ups: Great for back and biceps. Overhead Press: Focuses on shoulders and triceps. Rows: Strengthens the back.

2. Isolation Exercises:

Bicep Curls: For biceps. Tricep Extensions: Targets triceps. Leg Curls/Extensions: Focuses on hamstrings and quadriceps. Calf Raises: Strengthens calves.

Tips: Progressive Overload: Gradually increase the weight or resistance to keep challenging your muscles.
