MEAL PLANNING

Effective meal planning helps save time,reduce food waste,and maintain balanced nutrition.here are some tips:

  • Plan meals for the week,incorporating a variety of foods.
  • Use a grocerry list to avoid impulsive purchases
  • Prepare meals ahead and store them for convenience

    • HEALTHY EATING HABITS

    Establishing healthy eating habits ensures sustained energy and supports long term health.

    • Eat a balanced diet in fruits,vegetables,lean proteins and whole grains.
    • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
    • Limit sugar and processed foods.
    • Practice portion control

      • HEALTHY RECIPIES

      Try these simple,nutritious recipies to kickstart your journey:

      • Avacado Toast Mash avacado on whole grain toast,sprinkle with salt,pepper,and chilli flakes.
      • Greek Yougurt Parfait: Layer Yougurt with fruits,nuts,and a drizzle of honey for a balanced breakfast.
      • Veggie Stir-fry: Saute your favorite veggies with lean protein and serve over brown rice.
