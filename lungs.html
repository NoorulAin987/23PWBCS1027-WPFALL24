HOW TO GET HEALTHY LUNGS

To maintain healthy lungs ,focus on exercises that enhance respiratory strength and efficency .here are some effective exercises:

Cardio Workouts

Activities like running, brisk walking, cycling, and swimming improve lung capacity.

INTERVAL training

Alternating between high-intensity and low-intensity activities helps improve lung function.

BREATHING EXERCISE

Diaphragmatic Breathing: Deep breathing using the diaphragm. Pursed-Lip Breathing: Inhale through the nose and exhale slowly through pursed lips.
